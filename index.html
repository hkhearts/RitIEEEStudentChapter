About Us

Innovating the Future at Ramco Institute of Technology

The IEEE Computer Society Student Branch Chapter (STB11408) at Ramco Institute of Technology is a dynamic community of tech enthusiasts, developers, and researchers. We are dedicated to exploring cutting-edge technologies, from building custom compilers and resilient database engines to pioneering immersive WebXR experiences.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and high-level industry demands. Through hands-on hackathons, intensive workshops, competitive programming sessions, and collaborative projects, we empower students to transform their ideas into impactful, real-world solutions.
